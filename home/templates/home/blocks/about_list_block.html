2D/3D Animation

The development of an interesting concept and the design of gorgeous graphics is very important for the creation of a successful game, but those aren’t the only crucial factors. One always enjoys looking at a pretty picture, but it is the animation that brings it to life, making it interesting and – quite literally – moving. Through perfectly coordinated animations of various elements, we bring the graphics closer to reality and thereby to the viewer. Just looking at the game becomes an exciting experience, all thanks to our original animation design. It is indeed a moving experience.

Our 2D- and 3D-animation specialists have accumulated years of experience. They know exactly how to reflect light, how to turn objects or to move people in order to make them look more interesting and realistic. Starting with the animations of the simplest objects and "special effects" and through to complicated and realistic person animations – we can produce everything in high quality. Even if it’s just lightning striking in the background – we take every little task seriously and approach it with a high level of professionalism!

We have mastered a wide variety of development environments that we use to create our animations. The following ones are in demand amongst our partners: Maya, 3ds max, Fusion, Adobe After Effects, Spine and of course UNITY. As always, we are ready to accept new challenges and can quickly acquire a new "package" if necessary while animating.

3D Graphics
3D modeling & Texturing/ Skining & Rigging

A high-quality 3D model that meets all of the customer’s wishes and the technical requirements and characteristics – that’s the base that interesting animations, spectacular transitions and video clips are built on.

We have mastered the production of three-dimensional models in all of the most popular programs - max, maya, blender, zBrush, 3d coat. And, as our experience shows, we can easily master any new packages if necessary.

It’s not without reason that our slogan is “all can be created”. We really can create anything and everything and know no restrictions – neither in terms of the style nor the forms or types of 3D-models. Starting with simple objects through to complex characters, from high-field models for the production of video clips and to economical low-field models that can be used in online environments and on hardware with lower performance characteristics – we will always create exactly what you need!

An important stage of working with 3D models, but unfortunately one which is practically invisible to the viewer, is the preparation of those models for their animation – skinning, rigging. Alas, it always remains “behind the scenes”, even though the quality of the character animation relies directly on their quality. It goes without saying that this work is done in accordance to the highest standards.

Unity FX
Concepts & Layout Design / Sketches & Drawing

As already mentioned in the section about 3D-modelling, working directly in the UNITY engine is one of many skills we have perfected. Starting with the creation of simple objects right through to complex scenes, animations and gorgeous visual effects – our specialists are unsurpassed masters in this field. This is known and appreciated by our partners who are increasingly resorting to developments in this particular engine, including IGT – one of the leading companies in the industry. For them, we develop most of our projects in this particular environment.
